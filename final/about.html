About L's Web Design

L's Web Design had very humble beginnings. All the way back in 1888, my family created a local newspaper company, The Iris Paper. As the years have progressed, it has been everything from a newspaper to a co-op of businesses designed to bring information to others. Now it is my time to take over the business and what better way than continuing the tradition of helping people spread their message. To say the least, it has been a long road, but let us be your destination!
